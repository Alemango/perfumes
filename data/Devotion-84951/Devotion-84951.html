Devotion by Dolce&Gabbana is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women. This is a new fragrance. Devotion was launched in 2023. The nose behind this fragrance is Olivier Cresp. Top note is Candied Lemon; middle notes are Panacotta, Orange Blossom and Rum; base note is Vanilla. 
Devotion  is a celebration of the irresistible Italian dessert, panettone, and its delectable aromas. With top notes of candied lemon, a heart of orange blossom, and a base of vanilla, it's an enchanting gourmand blend, said Olivier Cresp.
